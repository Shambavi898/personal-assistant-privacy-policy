Privacy Policy for ProductivitySuite

Last Updated: 11.43 on 30-07-2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ProductivitySuite ("the App") handles your information. This Android Progressive Web App (PWA) is designed for local productivity management and does not require login or transmit user data to external servers.

1. Data Collection & Storage

The App does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on any server. All user-generated content (notes, tasks, budgets, etc.) is stored locally on your device using:

  • IndexedDB (browser-based storage)
  • Browser cache (for offline functionality)

No data leaves your device unless you explicitly export/share it.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads via Google AdMob, which may collect:

  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • Device model & OS version
  • Non-precise location (country-level)
  • IP address (for ad fraud prevention)

AdMob serves up to 5 unskippable interstitial ads during app use. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Opt-out of personalized ads: Adjust settings in your Google account (adssettings.google.com).

3. Data Deletion

To delete all app data:

  1. Clear browser storage (via device settings > Apps > [Browser] > Storage > Clear Data)
  2. Uninstall the PWA
  3. Alternatively, use the in-app "Reset Data" option (if available)

4. Security

Your data remains on your device with no server transmission. However:

  • We recommend device-level security (PIN, encryption)
  • No warranty is provided for local data loss
